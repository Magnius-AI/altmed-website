## Channel Strategy

### 1. Google Ads (40% of budget = $150-200/mo)
**Why:** People actively searching for services = highest intent

**Campaign 1: DOT Physicals**
- Keywords: "DOT physical Manassas", "CDL physical near me", "DOT exam Northern Virginia"
- Budget: $75/mo
- Expected: 20-30 clicks/mo → 5-10 appointments

**Campaign 2: Weight Loss**
- Keywords: "medical weight loss Manassas", "semaglutide clinic Virginia", "Wegovy doctor near me"
- Budget: $75/mo
- Expected: 15-25 clicks/mo → 3-8 consultations

**Campaign 3: Urgent Care (brand awareness)**
- Keywords: "walk-in clinic Manassas", "urgent care near me"
- Budget: $50/mo
- Expected: 30-50 clicks/mo → 10-15 visits

### 2. Facebook/Instagram Ads (30% = $100-150/mo)
**Why:** Build awareness, target demographics

**Ad Set 1: Weight Loss (Women 35-60)**
- Targeting: Manassas + 15 miles, women, interest in fitness/weight loss
- Creative: Before/after results, doctor credibility
- CTA: "Book Free Consultation"

**Ad Set 2: Families/Primary Care**
- Targeting: Parents, household income markers
- Creative: "Your neighborhood doctor" messaging
- CTA: "Same-Day Appointments Available"

**Ad Set 3: B2B - Trucking Companies**
- Targeting: Small business owners, trucking/logistics interests
- Creative: "DOT Compliance Made Easy"
- CTA: "Get Corporate Pricing"

### 3. Google Business Profile (Free)
**Actions:**
- Post weekly (office photos, health tips, promotions)
- Respond to ALL reviews within 24 hours
- Add services with descriptions
- Upload new photos monthly
- Use Q&A to answer common questions

### 4. Social Media Organic (Free)
**Posting Schedule:**
- Monday: Health tip
- Wednesday: Service spotlight
- Friday: Patient success story (with permission) or staff feature
- Occasional: Behind-the-scenes, community events

**Content Ideas:**
- "5 things to bring to your DOT physical"
- "How semaglutide works" (educational)
- "Meet our team: [Staff member]"
- Weight loss transformations (with permission)
- Local event sponsorships

### 5. Email Marketing (Low cost)
**Build list via:**
- Website popup (already created)
- Patient check-in signup
- "Get 10% off" cards at front desk

**Email Sequences:**
- Welcome series (4 emails over 2 weeks)
- Monthly newsletter (health tips + promotions)
- Reactivation ("We miss you" for inactive patients)
- Seasonal (flu shots, back-to-school physicals)
