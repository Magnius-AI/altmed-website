# Email Signup Popup Design

## Copy Options

### Option A: Health Tips Focus
**Headline:** "Get Free Health Tips From Our Doctors"
**Subhead:** Join 500+ Manassas families who get weekly wellness advice
**Button:** "Send Me Tips"
**Fields:** Email only (reduce friction)

### Option B: Discount Focus  
**Headline:** "First Visit? Get 10% Off"
**Subhead:** New patients save on their first appointment
**Button:** "Claim My Discount"
**Fields:** Email, First Name

### Option C: Weight Loss Lead Magnet
**Headline:** "Free: 7-Day Metabolism Boost Guide"
**Subhead:** Doctor-approved tips to kickstart your weight loss
**Button:** "Download Free Guide"
**Fields:** Email, First Name

---

## Popup Settings (Hostinger)

```
Trigger: Exit intent OR 30 seconds on page
Show: Once per visitor (cookie 7 days)
Pages: All except /contact and /appointment
Mobile: Bottom banner instead of popup (less intrusive)
```

---

## Hostinger Implementation

1. Go to Hostinger Website Builder → Add Elements → Popup
2. Or use free tool: **OptinMonster** (free tier) or **Sumo** (free)
3. Connect to email service (Mailchimp free tier works)

## Email Welcome Sequence (After Signup)

**Email 1 (Immediate):** "Welcome! Here's what to expect"
- Introduce clinic
- Link to book appointment
- List services

**Email 2 (Day 3):** "5 Signs You Need a Checkup"
- Health education
- Soft CTA to book

**Email 3 (Day 7):** "Special Offer Inside"
- 10% off first visit
- Urgency: expires in 48 hours

**Email 4 (Day 14):** "Meet Dr. Lee"
- Personal story
- Builds trust
